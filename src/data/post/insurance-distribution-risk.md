Embedded insurance and insurance-adjacent fintech models should be reviewed for who is selling, advising, referring,
administering, collecting information, and communicating with customers.

## Why structure matters

Distribution models can raise licensing, disclosure, compensation, marketing, and partner responsibility questions. The
contractual structure should reflect the actual customer experience.
