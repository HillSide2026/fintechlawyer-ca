MSB registration timing depends on the business model, readiness of compliance materials, ownership information,
product launch sequencing, and whether banking or processor partners require evidence of regulatory planning.

## Practical sequence

Teams usually need to understand the activity first: fund flows, customer roles, custody, foreign exchange, remittance
features, virtual currency activity, and the role of each partner in the payment chain.
