Payment platforms and embedded finance companies should assess MSB risk by looking at the real activity being performed:
who receives funds, who controls movement, who interfaces with customers, and who carries compliance responsibility.

## Why structure matters

Processor relationships do not automatically remove regulatory analysis. Product architecture, flow of funds, settlement,
custody, customer-facing representations, and contractual allocation of roles can all matter.
