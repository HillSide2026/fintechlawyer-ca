fintechlawyer.ca is a practical information site about Canadian fintech legal and regulatory topics.

## Information You Provide

If you contact the site, you may provide information such as your name, email address, company, and message content.
Do not submit confidential information unless a formal engagement process has been completed.

## Site Data

The site may collect ordinary technical information generated by browsers and hosting infrastructure, such as IP address,
browser type, pages visited, referring pages, and timestamps.

## Use Of Information

Information may be used to respond to messages, improve site content, maintain security, and understand which topics are
useful to readers.

## No Sale Of Personal Information

fintechlawyer.ca does not sell personal information.
